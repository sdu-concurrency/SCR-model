Step-by-step instructions for participants on how to access and complete the Supply Chain Resilience survey.

🎯 Overview

What is the Supply Chain Resilience Survey?

The Supply Chain Resilience Assessment Tool helps organizations identify vulnerabilities and assess capabilities across their supply chain. As a participant, you'll complete a structured survey that assesses your organization's resilience in 7 key areas.

Survey Structure

The survey consists of 7 steps covering:

Step Area Description
1 Job Function & Company Your role and organization details
2 Vulnerability Ranking Rank top 10 vulnerabilities for your organization
3 Finance Assess financial capabilities
4 Customers / Demand Assess customer and demand-related capabilities
5 Products / Processes Assess product and process capabilities
6 Inventory Management Assess inventory and stock capabilities
7 Sourcing / Purchasing & Systems & Management Assess sourcing, systems, and management capabilities

Time Required

The survey typically takes 20-30 minutes to complete, depending on your familiarity with the topics. You can save your progress and return later.

🔗 Accessing the Survey

Step-by-step

  1. Receive your link from your session manager

    The link will look like: /session/name/survey?u=your-user&t=your-token

  2. Open the link in your web browser

    You can use Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, or any modern browser.

  3. View the Welcome page

    You'll see a welcome message with information about the survey.

  4. Click "Go to survey form" to begin

🔐 No Password Required

Your survey link contains a unique token, so you don't need to remember a password. Simply click the link provided by your session manager and you're in!

⚠️ Important

Do not share your link with others. Each link is unique to one participant and allows access to your specific survey responses.

📋 Survey Steps

Step 1: Job Function & Company Information

  1. Select your job function from the dropdown (Purchase, Production, Sales, Logistics, Management, etc.)
  2. Enter your company name
  3. Click Next to continue

Step 2: Vulnerability Ranking

In this step, you'll identify the top 10 vulnerabilities most relevant to your organization.

  1. You'll see 7 categories of potential vulnerabilities:

    • Finance
    • Customers / demand
    • Processes / organization
    • Systems / data
    • Purchasing / sourcing
    • Supply chain end-to-end
    • Environment

  2. Drag and drop to rank the top 10 most relevant vulnerabilities for your organization

  3. Order matters - rank from most important (#1) to least (#10)

  4. Click Next to continue

💡 Tip

Think about which vulnerabilities have the greatest impact on your organization's supply chain resilience. Consider both likelihood and severity.

Steps 3-7: Capability Assessments

Each step covers a different capability area. You'll rate your organization's capability level in each area.

Step Area Focus
3 Finance Financial reserves, credit access, risk management
4 Customers / Demand Customer diversification, demand forecasting, relationship management
5 Products / Processes Product standardization, process flexibility, lean operations, cross-training
6 Inventory Management Safety stock levels, inventory visibility, buffer strategy
7 Sourcing / Purchasing & Systems & Management Multi-sourcing, supplier qualification, IT systems, executive commitment, business continuity

Rating Scale

For each item, rate the capability level:

  • 1 - Low capability (We don't have this capability or it's very weak)
  • 2 - Basic capability (We have some capability but it's limited)
  • 3 - Good capability (We have solid capability in this area)
  • 4 - Excellent capability (We have strong, well-developed capability)

💡 Tip

Be honest in your assessments. The goal is to identify both strengths and areas for improvement. There are no "wrong" answers.

✅ Submitting

After completing Step 7

  1. Review your responses (you can navigate back to any step)
  2. Click Submit to finalize your responses
  3. You'll see a confirmation page
  4. Your session manager will be notified of your completion

📧 What happens next?

Once all participants in your session have completed their surveys, your session manager will review the aggregated results and share insights with the group. The analysis will show:

  • Which vulnerabilities were ranked highest across the organization
  • Capability levels across different areas
  • Comparison charts for cross-organizational alignment
  • Actionable recommendations for improvement

⚠️ Cannot edit after submission

Once you submit your survey, you cannot go back and change your responses. Make sure to review all your answers before clicking Submit.

💾 Saving Progress

Auto-save

The form automatically saves your progress as you go. If you:

  • Close your browser
  • Lose your internet connection
  • Need to take a break

...your answers will be restored when you return using the same link.

Browser Requirements

  • JavaScript must be enabled for the auto-save to work
  • Use a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  • Don't use "Incognito" or "Private" mode if you want to save progress

💾 Where is my data saved?

Your survey progress is saved in your browser's local storage. This means:

  • Your data stays on your computer
  • You can continue from any device if you use the same browser
  • Your session manager cannot see incomplete responses until you submit

💡 Tips & Best Practices

Before Starting

  • Set aside dedicated time - 20-30 minutes without interruptions
  • Gather information - Have relevant data about your organization handy
  • Understand the context - Think about your entire supply chain, not just your department

During the Survey

  • Be honest - There are no right or wrong answers
  • Take your time - Thoughtful responses lead to better insights
  • Use the full scale - Don't be afraid to use both low and high ratings
  • Save your link - Bookmark the survey link in case you need to return later

If You Get Stuck

  • Contact your session manager - They can provide clarification
  • Take a break - Your progress is saved automatically
  • Check your token - If your link doesn't work, ask for a new one

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What if I lose my survey link?

Contact your session manager. They can generate a new token and send you a fresh link.

Can I change my answers after submitting?

No, once you submit your survey, your responses are final. This ensures data integrity for the analysis. Make sure to review all your answers before submitting.

What if I don't understand a question?

Each question includes a label/description. If you're still unsure, contact your session manager for clarification.

How long does the survey take?

Typically 20-30 minutes, but this can vary depending on your familiarity with the topics and the complexity of your organization's supply chain.

Can I use my phone or tablet?

Yes, the survey is responsive and works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices. However, we recommend using a desktop or laptop for the best experience, especially for the drag-and-drop vulnerability ranking step.

What if I need to stop and come back later?

No problem! Your progress is saved automatically. Just use the same link to return and continue where you left off.

Will my individual responses be shared?

Your individual responses are confidential. The session manager can only see aggregated results across all participants. Your name and specific responses are not shared with other participants.

What happens to my data?

Your survey responses are stored securely in the system's database. They are used solely for the purpose of supply chain resilience assessment and are not shared with third parties.

Can I skip a question?

The survey requires responses for all questions before you can submit. If you're unsure about a particular question, provide your best estimate.
