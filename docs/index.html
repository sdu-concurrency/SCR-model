Welcome to the Supply Chain Resilience Software

On these pages you can find guides for the different user roles in the Supply Chain Resilience Software. The different roles are 1) administrator, 2) session manager, and 3) participant in a session.

Velkommen til Supply Chain Resilience softwaret

På disse sider kan du finde vejledninger for de forskellige brugerroller i Supply Chain Resilience softwaret. De forskellige roller er 1) administrator, 2) sessionsleder og 3) deltager i en session.

Overview

The Supply Chain Resilience Software helps participants identify vulnerabilities and capabilities in their organization's supply chains. Vulnerabilities are the fundamental factors that make an organization susceptible to disruptions. Capabilities are the properties/skills that enable an organization to anticipate and overcome disruptions. The process unfolds in four phases:

Phase 1: Map the Supply Chain

Understand the organization's supply chains through a mapping exercise in a cross-organizational team.

Phase 2: Identify Vulnerabilities and Capabilities

Participants individually identify vulnerabilities, which are risk-assessed, and subsequently assess capabilities to address them.

Phase 3: Prioritize and Create Cross-Organizational Alignment

Participants collectively identify vulnerabilities, which are risk-assessed, and subsequently assess capabilities to address them.

Phase 4: Prepare Action Plans

Concrete action plans are developed to reduce vulnerabilities and strengthen capabilities.

Oversigt

Supply Chain Resilience softwaret hjælper deltagerne med at identificere sårbarheder og kapabiliteter i virksomhedens forsyningskæder. Sårbarheder er de fundamentale faktorer, som gør en virksomhed følsom overfor forstyrrelser. Kapabiliteter er de egenskaber/evner, der gør en virksomhed i stand til at forudsige og overvinde forstyrrelser. Processen forløber i fire faser:

Fase 1: Kortlæg forsyningskæden

Forstå virksomhedens forsyningskæder via en kortlægningsøvelse i et tværorganisatorisk team.

Fase 2: Identificer sårbarheder og kapabiliteter

Deltagerne identificerer individuelt sårbarheder, som risikovurderes, og vurderer efterfølgende kapabiliteter til at håndtere sårbarhederne.

Fase 3: Prioriter og skab tværorganisatorisk alignment

Deltagerne identificerer samlet sårbarheder, som risikovurderes, og vurderer efterfølgende kapabiliteter til at håndtere sårbarhederne.

Fase 4: Udarbejd handleplaner

Der udarbejdes konkrete handleplaner til at mindske sårbarheder og styrke kapabiliteter.

About the Project

Project Background

This tool is developed as part of the Cybersikkerhed og Forretningskontinuitet (Cybersecurity and Business Continuity) project, funded by the Danish Industry Foundation (Industriens Fond) and carried out by researchers from the University of Southern Denmark (SDU) in partnership with the Royal Danish Military Academy (Forsvarsakademiet).

Project NameCybersikkerhed og Forretningskontinuitet
Project OwnerSyddansk Universitet (University of Southern Denmark)
Period2023–2026
Funded byIndustriens Fond
Project Websitecyber-smv.dk
Project LeaderJan Stentoft, Professor, SDU – stentoft@sam.sdu.dk

The project integrates cybersecurity into supply chain resilience work for Danish SMEs. It builds directly on the earlier Supply Chain Resilience project (2021–2023).
